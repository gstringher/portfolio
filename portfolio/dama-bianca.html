dama bianca

/ info

Dama Bianca is a mobile video game app inspired by folklore myths of Duino, in Friuli-Venezia-Giulia: we combined these stories to create a hybrid game between the famous Doodle Jump and the relaxing Fern Flower.

the story

The legend of the White Lady, widespread across many cultures, tells the story of a woman who, after a violent death, transformed into a rock formation resembling a human figure, visible beneath Duino Castle in Friuli-Venezia Giulia. Since then, her spirit is said to wander the castle. In the video game inspired by the legend, the story is reimagined: the White Lady survives and has an illegitimate son, Bepi, who embarks on a journey to find her. The game, similar to "Doodle Jump", involves collecting fragments of the white rock (diamonds) by jumping between platforms and unlocking characters who assist Bepi in his quest.
