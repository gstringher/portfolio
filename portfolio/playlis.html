/ brief

The project focused on the theme of media literacy, highlighting the impact of the rapid spread of digital technologies and the challenges in monitoring and regulating their use. This phenomenon often led to users misusing online tools. The brief required leveraging digital media as educational tools to promote a more conscious and responsible use of technology.
